# After setup
After you have set up docker and portainer (https://homelabdocs.deslimbos.nl/en/raspberrypi), you have to log into your portainer dashboard and add a network. The defaults are good you just have to name it "homelab-network"
After that you can use whatever stacks you want. https://homelabdocs.deslimbos.nl/e/en/raspberrypi/portainerdocker/stacks
